FAQ

How do I log a meditation session?

Tap the + button to create a new entry. Set your duration, mood, stress level, and add an optional reflection.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your personal information.

Can I edit past entries?

Yes, tap any entry to view and edit its details.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Data Collection

Lugn does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your meditation entries, mood ratings, stress levels, and reflections are stored locally on your device.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics
  • No location data
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party tracking

Health Data

If you choose to connect Lugn with Apple Health, meditation sessions are saved to HealthKit on your device. This data is controlled by Apple's privacy policies and never leaves your device through our app.

Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

Your data remains on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.
